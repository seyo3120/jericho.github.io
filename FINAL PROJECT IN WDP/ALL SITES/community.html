What is the best way to calm an anxious dog?

Is it helpful for you?

ANSWER:
Try giving him a toy or a piece of clothing with your scent before you leave. This can comfort him and help reduce anxiety. You might also try leaving a caming treat or using an anxiety wrap like a Thundershirt.

Why does my cat insist on sleeping on my keyboard? It's so annoying!

Is it helpful for you?

ANSWER:
Cats love warm, cozy spots, and your keyboard might be just the right temperature and texture for a nap. Don't worry, it's not personal! They're just being cats.

My dog barks at every friend and visitor who comes to my house. How do I stop this?

Is it helpful for you?

ANSWER:
Your dog might be barking because they see visitors as a threat to their territory. Try getting them used to new people gradually, and teach them a 'quiet' command. Reward calm behavior around guests, and with time, they'll learn to be more relaxed.
